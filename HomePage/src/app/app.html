LABAID Specialized Hospital

Labaid Specialized Hospital is one of the leading tertiary care hospitals in the country having a high reputation for its clinical excellence and services since 2006. Equipped with modern theatre facilities, ICU, NICU, HDU and team of renowned surgeons, consultants and other physicians, well trained and devoted nursing team and support team, the hospital offers state of the art personalized care and services. For effective healthcare services, it has become the hospital of choice for a large section of population of the country.

Currently, major services are as follows:

  1. General and Laparoscopic Surgery
  2. Gynae Surgery
  3. Orthopedic Surgery
  4. Neuro Surgery
  5. Urology
  6. Nephrology
  7. Medicine
  8. ICU
  9. NICU

Inpatient facilities

  1. Executive Cabins
  2. Deluxe Cabins
  3. High Care Beds
  4. Twin Share Cabins
  5. 14 General ICU
  6. HDU
  7. 8 NICU
  8. Pre & Post Operative
  9. 8 Modern Equipped OT
  10. IVF

Outpatient facilities

  1. Ambulance & Mobile Critical Care Fleet
  2. Outpatient Surgery Service
  3. Investigation facilities (Radiology, Pathology & Non-Lab Services)
  4. 24/7 Pharmacy
  5. Consultation facilities
